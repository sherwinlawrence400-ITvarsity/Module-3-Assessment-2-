Orio Milkshake

Price R 30.00 (500ml)

Prep time: 10 mins

Yield: 2

Ingredients

6 orios, crushed
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 pint vanilla ice cream
1 cup milk

Steps

  1. Blend 6 Orios, 1/4 tsp. vanilla, 1 cup ice cream and 4 Tbsp chocolate syrup. milk in blender until smooth.

  2. Pour into 2 glasses. Repeat with remaining orios, vanilla, ice cream and milk. Serve immediately.
