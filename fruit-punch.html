Fruit Punch

Price R 30.00 (500ml)

Prep time: 10 mins

Yield: 1

Ingredients

6 ounce can of frozen lemonade, thawed
6 ounce can of frozen limeade, thawed
6 ounce can of frozen orange juice, thawed
1 quart Club soda
1 quart Ginger Ale

Just before serving, add:


3 quarts 7 UP
Lime slices or small wedges

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients except 7 UP and lime slices

  2. Refrigerate until ready to serve

  3. Add 7 UP and float thinly sliced lime or small wedges to punch bowl or pitcher as shown
