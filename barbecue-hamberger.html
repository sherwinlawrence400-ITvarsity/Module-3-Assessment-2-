Barbecue Hamberger

R 45.00

Prep time: 15 mins

Yield: 4

Ingredients

4 hamburger patties,preferably natural beef
4 hamburger buns
butter
Mayonnaise
4 slices of swiss cheese
4 slices of chedder cheese
8 strips cooked bacon (2 for each burger)
lettuce leaves
4 slices of tomato (1 per burger)
sliced onion (optional)
Whiskey barbecue sauce

Steps

  1. Begin grilling the hamburger patties to the correct doneness
  2. While the burgers are cooking, prepare the buns by buttering both sides lightly and warming or broiling them
  3. When the buns are warmed, place them side by side
  4. Spread mayonnaise on the top half of each bun and top with lettuce and tomato
  5. When you turn the hamburger patties, place 1 slice of Swiss and 1 slice of cheddar on the patty and then top each with two slices of cooked bacon
  6. Spread the Whiskey Barbecue Sauce on the bottom half of each bun and then top with a burger patty
  7. Place burgers on a platter and serve with my potato salad
